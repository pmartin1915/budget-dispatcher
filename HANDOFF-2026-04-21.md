## Self-improvement trajectory

### The loop that already exists

`sandbox-workflow-enhancement` IS the self-improvement mechanism. The PC is already dispatching proposals, self-audits, and roadmap reviews on it. The firewall: it writes proposals to `docs/proposals/`, never edits the live dispatcher.

### What's missing to close the loop

1. **Merge tracker** — Auto branches are created but no signal flows back from "Perry merged it" or "Perry deleted it." Need `scripts/track-merges.mjs` that watches `auto/*` branches and records merge/close/stale status.

2. **Value ledger** — After each dispatch, log `files_changed`, `lines_added`, `lines_removed`, `branch`, `commit_sha` in the JSONL entry. Worker.mjs `verifyAndCommit()` already has the git context. ~30 lines.

3. **Selector feedback** — Feed merge-rate per (project, taskClass) into the selector prompt. "audit tasks on burn-wizard have 60% merge rate. refactor on sandbox has 10%." The selector learns to prefer high-value work.

4. **Prompt evolution** — The sandbox-workflow-enhancement proposals should include prompt refinement proposals. The dispatcher proposes better selector/worker/audit prompts for itself, Perry reviews, merged prompts take effect.

## Worldbuilder integration design

The worldbuilder needs a different task vocabulary than code projects:

| Task | Type | What it does | Safety |
|------|------|-------------|--------|
| `lore-audit` | Read-only | Check content for internal contradictions | Safe |
| `naming-validate` | Read-only | Verify names follow ADR-0004 linguistic rules | Safe |
| `consistency-check` | Read-only | Cross-ref between civs, traditions, deities | Safe |
| `slot-fill` | Write | Populate mechanical details from templates | Needs creative gate |
| `research-synthesis` | Write (docs) | Run deep research prompts, produce summaries | Low risk |

**Creative gate** (parallel to clinical gate): Any autonomous write to worldbuilder content must be audited for canon consistency. Gemini reads the relevant ADRs + existing canon, flags contradictions. Critical findings → revert.

**Key constraint from memory:** Perry hand-authors the creative spine. The dispatcher fills in mechanical details and validates consistency. It never invents major lore, names civilizations, or creates new deities. That's Perry's domain.

**Repos:** worldbuilder (framework, committed) + veydria-atlas (data). Dispatcher works on veydria-atlas data files, not the framework.
